About Me

My name is Chen Li. I am a first-generation immigrant from China. I was born in Chengdu, Sichuan province. And I came to Utah when I was eight years old. I graduated from the University of Utah in 2009 with a Bachelors of Science in Chemical Engineer, and met my wife the following year in a study abroad in Japan. Since then, I have accumulated 5 years of experiences working in QA data analysis and SAP QA module sysadmin positions.


I currently work at a company called MasterControl, whose software is primarily Document Control, but whose application extends to Medical and Pharmaceutical industries. I love my job but also would love to expand my skillset to include web development and coding.

My interests and hobbies include:

  • Reading
  • Watching movies and tv shows
  • Hiking and taking walks in nature
  • Playing video games
  • Playing board games
  • Being a general social butterfly with the people that like any of the above things

My current career goals are, in order of priorities:

  1. Excel at my position as a software quality engineer
  2. Complete the web developer's boot camp
  3. Apply/practice my learned knowledge in a work environment
  4. Seek ways to expand my professional responsibilities with my expanded skillset
  5. Become the master of my own destiny!
